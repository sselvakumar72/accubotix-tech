The Problem We're Solving
Pipelines fail silently. doesn't let them.

THE OLD WAY

  • Manual inspection teams access one segment at a time
  • Insulation removed, inspected, re-installed — weeks of downtime
  • No detection until corrosion is already severe
  • Human error, inconsistent data, no historical record
  • Reactive maintenance — fix what already broke
  • High cost, high risk, low coverage

THE WAY

  • Autonomous crawler traverses live pipelines continuously
  • Inspects through insulation — zero removal required
  • Detects defects at sub-millimetre precision, in real time
  • Every scan geotagged, timestamped, stored in digital twin
  • Predictive failure modelling — act before damage occurs
  • Fraction of the cost. Zero unplanned shutdowns.
Why
Three sensors. One truth.

Most inspection tools see one thing. fuses visual, thermal, and acoustic data through a Bayesian AI engine — cross-validating every finding to eliminate false positives and deliver a ranked, actionable repair list.

👁

Visual AI

360° 4K ring camera with CNN-powered crack and corrosion detection. Catches surface anomalies invisible to the human eye at speed.

🌡

Thermal Sensing

LWIR microbolometer detects leaks and blockages through insulation by mapping temperature differentials as small as 1°C — no stripping required.

📡

Acoustic Emission

Dual-mode AE + ultrasonic sensor pinpoints active cracks via TDOA localisation and measures wall thickness to ±0.1mm, catching hidden corrosion early.

⚡

Edge AI

NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX processes all three sensor streams onboard at >5 FPS per module. No cloud dependency. No latency. Results in real time.

🗄

Digital Asset Twin

Every defect tagged to a Segment ID with precise distance markers. Full material, age, and repair history per pipe section — enabling predictive RUL modelling.

📊

Auto-Reports + CMMS

PDF inspection reports auto-generated post-run with ranked repair priorities. Direct CMMS/ERP work order triggers on threshold breach. Zero manual admin.
