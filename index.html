Why AccuBotix
Three sensors. One truth.

Most inspection tools see one thing. AccuBotix fuses visual, thermal, and acoustic data through a Bayesian AI engine — cross-validating every finding to eliminate false positives and deliver a ranked, actionable repair list.

👁

Visual AI

360° 4K ring camera with CNN-powered crack and corrosion detection. Catches surface anomalies invisible to the human eye at speed.

🌡

Thermal Sensing

LWIR microbolometer detects leaks and blockages through insulation by mapping temperature differentials as small as 1°C — no stripping required.

📡

Acoustic Emission

Dual-mode AE + ultrasonic sensor pinpoints active cracks via TDOA localisation and measures wall thickness to ±0.1mm, catching hidden corrosion early.

⚡

Edge AI

NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX processes all three sensor streams onboard at >5 FPS per module. No cloud dependency. No latency. Results in real time.

🗄

Digital Asset Twin

Every defect tagged to a Segment ID with precise distance markers. Full material, age, and repair history per pipe section — enabling predictive RUL modelling.

📊

Auto-Reports + CMMS

PDF inspection reports auto-generated post-run with ranked repair priorities. Direct CMMS/ERP work order triggers on threshold breach. Zero manual admin.
