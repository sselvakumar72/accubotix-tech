Why we started

Industrial inspection often depends on manual access, expert interpretation, fragmented data, and delayed reporting. We believe critical assets deserve better visibility.

Technologix was founded in Bengaluru by engineers focused on practical automation for industrial asset health. Our work centers on robots that can move through challenging facilities, capture high-quality sensor data, and convert that data into decisions maintenance teams can trust.

The company theme is simple: Automated Inspection. The goal is not automation for its own sake, but precision in every inspection step: route execution, sensor capture, evidence mapping, anomaly detection, and report generation.

Our platform approach allows new inspection products to be added over time while sharing a common foundation of mobility, sensing, AI-assisted analytics, digital asset records, and operator-ready reports.

Every product is shaped by field usability: reducing manual risk, improving repeatability, preserving inspection history, and helping teams act before small issues become operational disruptions.

What We Stand For

Precision First

Every inspection workflow is designed around measurable evidence, repeatable capture, and clear interpretation. Precision is the standard behind every innovation we build.
