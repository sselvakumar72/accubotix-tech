Why we started

The same question kept coming up across India's refinery and petrochemical sector. Why do we keep discovering corrosion after it's already caused damage?

was founded in Bengaluru by engineers who spent years working on industrial inspection challenges across India's refinery and petrochemical sector. The same question came up again and again: why are we still discovering corrosion after it's already caused damage?

The answer wasn't a better probe — it was a smarter, autonomous system that could traverse live pipelines, fuse multiple sensor streams, and give operators a ranked, actionable repair list before failures occurred. That insight became .

Today, we are building and testing the AX-1 crawler against a purpose-built 4-environment test facility — physical rig, full crawler track, sensor integration lab, and digital twin simulation — designed to meet the demands of oil & gas, refinery, and industrial plant operators across South and Southeast Asia.

Our test program starts with a physics-accurate Gazebo simulation, moves to engineered-defect physical rig validation, integrates the full Jetson AI pipeline, and culminates in full-system crawler track runs through all four challenge zones. Every design decision is validated against ground truth before we move to the next phase.

What We Stand For
Four principles. No exceptions.

Physics First

Every design decision is grounded in sensor physics, materials science, and mechanical reality — not marketing claims. We test against engineered defects with known ground truth and publish the results.

Zero Excuses on Accuracy

±0.1 mm wall thickness resolution. ≥1°C thermal sensitivity. TDOA crack localisation. We set hard accuracy targets and build until we hit them — not until they look impressive on a spec sheet.

Operator-Centric Design

Inspection data is only valuable if operators act on it. Our platform is built around the people who run plants — not data scientists. Reports are clear. Priorities are ranked. Work orders fire automatically.

Built for India, Ready for the World

Designed and manufactured in Bengaluru. Engineered for the heat, humidity, and operating conditions of South Asian industrial environments — and exportable globally from day one.
