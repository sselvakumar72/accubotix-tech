Robotic Partial Discharge Inspection

Autonomous Electrical Asset Health Monitoring

Partial discharge is an early sign of insulation deterioration in medium-voltage and high-voltage assets. If it is not detected and addressed in time, PD activity can progress into flashover, cable failure, switchgear damage, transformer outage, fire risk, and unplanned plant downtime.
