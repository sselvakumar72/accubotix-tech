01

Crawler Platform

The crawler is a 4WD spring-loaded chassis engineered for DN100 cast iron pipelines. Independent spring-preloaded wheels maintain constant grip through the aluminium cladding — even on 30° inclines and 1 m vertical risers. An articulated hinge mechanism allows the chassis to flex through 90° and 45° bends without wheel slip or sensor head collision.

Odometry is handled by quad encoder wheels combined with MPU-9250 IMU dead reckoning, with RFID absolute position resets at every pipe joint for sub-centimetre location accuracy across the full 3.5-hour inspection run.

4WD Spring-Wheel Drive DN100 Cast Iron 90° + 45° Bend Traversal 30° Incline Rated 1 m Vertical Riser RFID Odometry Reset MPU-9250 IMU 3.5 hr Battery Life
02

Trimodal Sensor Suite

Three independently validated sensing modalities mounted on a single crawler head, capturing data simultaneously. The 4K 360° visual ring detects surface cracks and corrosion in real time. The LWIR microbolometer (8–14 μm) reads thermal signatures through intact insulation to reveal leaks, blockages, and corrosion-under-insulation (CUI) hot spots — no stripping required.

The dual-mode acoustic emission + ultrasonic sensor runs TDOA source localisation for active crack detection and pulse-echo thickness gauging for sub-surface wall loss at ±0.1 mm resolution — all three modalities running in parallel, all in real time.

4K 360° Visual Ring LWIR 8–14 μm ≥1°C Thermal Sensitivity 40 kHz AE Sensor ±0.1 mm Wall Thickness TDOA Crack Localisation CUI Detection
03

Edge AI — Jetson Orin NX

Three AI modules run in parallel on the NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX 8GB onboard. A CNN vision model analyses the visual stream at >5 FPS. A statistical thermal anomaly engine processes the FLIR feed. An FFT + LSTM classifier interprets acoustic emission patterns for crack and leak signatures.

All three outputs feed into a Bayesian sensor fusion engine that cross-validates detections. A finding confirmed by two or more modalities is elevated to the repair priority list. A single-modality hit is flagged for human review. The result: near-zero false positives and zero cloud dependency during inspection. Dual 512 GB industrial microSD cards provide mirrored, encrypted storage — a single card failure cannot lose data.

Bayesian Sensor Fusion CNN Vision Model Statistical Thermal Engine FFT + LSTM Acoustic >5 FPS per Module NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX 8GB Dual 512 GB Mirrored Storage ROS 2 Humble
04

Cloud Pipeline & Digital Twin

Post-run, the crawler offloads 3.5 hours of triple-sensor recording to AWS S3 or Azure Blob via Wi-Fi in under 30 minutes. A GPU inference pipeline processes the data and attaches Segment ID metadata to every defect record — linking precise pipe location to every finding.

The web dashboard surfaces live alerts, auto-generated PDF inspection reports, and CMMS/ERP work order triggers on threshold breach. Each pipe segment builds its own digital twin: material grade, age, operating temperature, full defect history, and a remaining-useful-life (RUL) estimate updated after every inspection run — enabling predictive maintenance at the asset level.

Middleware

ROS 2 Humble

Core robot middleware — identical stack used in simulation and on the physical crawler. Sensor topics, navigation commands, and AI module interfaces are identical in both environments.

Physics Simulation

Gazebo Harmonic

3D physics simulation with accurate friction coefficients for DN100 cast iron pipe. CI bend sections, inclines, and the vertical riser are all modelled with real-world surface properties.

Navigation

Nav2 Stack

Autonomous path planning through 90° and 45° bends validated in simulation first. Dead-reckoning drift quantified, RFID reset accuracy tested, odometry error logged per metre.

Visualisation

RViz2

Real-time visualisation of sensor data, pose estimation, and defect detections during simulation runs. Used to validate Bayesian fusion output against injected defect ground truth.
