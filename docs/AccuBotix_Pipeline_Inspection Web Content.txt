                    CORE TECHNOLOGY 
The Science of Certain Inspection
Four integrated technology layers from physical sensing to cloud intelligence that are designed to operate as one unified system.
01 Crawler Platform 
•	The AccuBotix crawler is a 4WD spring-loaded chassis designed for DN100 cast iron pipelines.
•	Independent spring-loaded wheels maintain constant grip through aluminium cladding, even on 30° inclines and on vertical risers. 
•	An articulated hinge mechanism allows the crawler to move through 90° and 45° bends.
•	This helps prevent wheel slip and avoids sensor head collision during inspection. 
•	Odometry is managed using quad encoder wheels and MPU-9250 IMU dead reckoning. RFID position resets at every pipe joint, providing sub-centimeter location accuracy. This accuracy is maintained throughout the full 3.5-hour inspection run.

02  Trimodal Sensor Suite
Three independently validated sensing modalities are integrated into a single crawler head, capturing data simultaneously. The 4K 360° visual ring detects surface cracks and corrosion in real time.
The LWIR micro bolometer (8–14 μm) reads thermal signatures through intact insulation to detect leaks, blockages, and corrosion-under-insulation (CUI) hot spots.
*No stripping is required.
The dual-mode acoustic emission and ultrasonic sensor uses TDOA source localization to detect active cracks. It also performs pulse-echo thickness gauging to detect sub-surface wall loss at ±0.1 mm resolution. All three sensing modalities operate in parallel, delivering data in real time.
Sensor suite applications 
•	4K 360° Visual Ring Imaging
•	LWIR Thermal Sensing (8–14 μm)
•	≥1°C Thermal Sensitivity
•	40 kHz Acoustic Emission Sensor
•	±0.1 mm Wall Thickness Measurement
•	TDOA-Based Crack Localization
•	Corrosion-Under-Insulation (CUI) Detection
03 Edge AI - Jetson Orin NX 
        Three AI modules run in parallel on the NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX 8GB onboard system. A CNN vision model analyzes the visual stream at over 5 FPS. A statistical thermal anomaly engine processes the FLIR thermal feed. An FFT + LSTM classifier interprets acoustic emission patterns to identify crack and leak signatures. 
	All three outputs feed into a Bayesian sensor fusion engine for cross-validation of detections. Findings confirmed by two or more sensing modalities are elevated to the repair priority list. Single-modality detections are flagged for human review and further assessment. This approach delivers near-zero false positives with zero cloud dependency during inspection. Dual 512 GB industrial micro SD cards provide mirrored, encrypted storage for secure data protection. Even if a single card fails, inspection data remains protected and cannot be lost.

04 Cloud Principles & Digital Twin 
After each run, the crawler transfers 3.5 hours of triple sensor recorded data to AWS S3  or Cloud storage over Wi-Fi in under 30 minutes. A GPU inference pipeline processes the data and attaches Segment ID metadata to every defect record.
This links every finding to its precise pipe location. 	
The web dashboard displays live alerts and auto-generated PDF inspection reports, and CMMS/ERP work order triggers when thresholds are breached.  
	Each pipe segment maintains its own digital twin, including material grade, age, operating temperature, full defect history, and a remaining useful life (RUL) estimate updated after every inspection run. 
	This enables predictive maintenance at the asset level.
